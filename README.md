# DARIVS — Social Impact Prediction Platform

**Good Just Happens**

## What is DARIVS?

DARIVS is a Social Impact Prediction Platform that combines 
charitable giving with analytical engagement.

Users participate in social forecasting events, contribute 
to verified charitable funds, and earn rewards for analytical accuracy.

## How It Works

**Digital Participation Pack — 50/35/15 Model:**

- 35% → Direct charitable contribution to verified partner fund
- 50% → Expert Reward Pool (distributed to most accurate analysts)
- 15% → Platform service fee (ARVEN AI infrastructure)

This is not gambling. This is Social Impact Forecasting —
a Game of Skill where participants use AI-powered analytics 
to make informed predictions on social and humanitarian events.
